Machine Learning Project

Detecting pneumonia from X-Ray

We are a group of Biomedical Engineering students from UVic that have created a machine learning system which enables the detection of pneumonia from X-Ray images.

Pneumonia is an infection of the lung that can be caused by multiple microorganisms, such as bacteria, viruses and fungis. This illness is classified according to wether it is acquired: community-acquired pneumonia or hospital-acquired pneumonia. Anyone can get pneumonia, and there are many factors that determine that a person is more susceptible to infection by some microorganisms or others. In developed countries it is the sixth cause of death. Approximately 7 to 15 cases are observed for every 1000 people per year. In addition, it is the leading cause of death in children An early diagnosis is crucial, since it involves a progressive aggravation that in many cases ends people's life
